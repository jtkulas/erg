Ethanol-free petrol map

The map below contains information from 2818 stations in MN, WI, SD, ND, and IA indicating that they offer ethanol-free gas. The map data was taken from pure-gas.org, but also relies on latitude and longitude estimates generated from physical addresses via the (R Core Team 2021) package tidygeocoder (Cambon et al. 2021) version 1.0.3 as well as geocodio.
